Palandöken Panorama: Where Slopes Meet the Sky!

Palandöken, located in the eastern city of Erzurum, is Turkey's premier winter sports destination. Its blend of top-notch skiing facilities and captivating landscapes has made it a hotspot for both local and international winter sports enthusiasts.

Majestic Mountain Range

Boasting one of the longest ski runs in Turkey, Palandöken offers 28 slopes that stretch over 43 kilometers. With elevations ranging between 2,200 meters to 3,176 meters, skiers and snowboarders can revel in challenging descents, while also enjoying the panoramic views that the mountain offers.

Sunrise to Sunset Skiing

What sets Palandöken apart is its unique feature of day and night skiing. As the sun sets and the slopes light up, the mountain transforms into a winter wonderland, offering a surreal skiing experience under the starlit sky.

Diverse Delights

Palandöken isn't just about skiing and snowboarding. The mountain is home to various other winter sports such as snowkiting and ice climbing. Whether you're a novice or a seasoned adventurer, Palandöken promises an activity that suits your thrill level.

Rich Regional Reverie

Beyond the slopes, Palandöken is deeply entrenched in the rich history and culture of Erzurum. Visitors can explore the city's historical landmarks, indulge in local delicacies, and immerse themselves in the warm hospitality of the region.
